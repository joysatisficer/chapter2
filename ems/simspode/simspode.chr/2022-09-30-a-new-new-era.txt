## a new (new) era

Welcome to this place of madness. This is take 3 of my attempt at something like a blog. Last time, I tried to commit to just casting my thoughts out into the void (kind of like twitter, but longer, and more abstract, and not as subject to molochian social forces), but this failed horrifically and I wound up burning myself out by writing a book-length series of explanations on my view of the multiverse at the time (which was rather similar to [UDASSA](http://fennetic.net/irc/finney.org/~hal/udassa/)), and to top it all off, I changed said view before I could finish writing it down.

So welcome to the third iteration. This time, I'll actively try to stay away from writing monolithic posts thousands of words long. And I'll try to be more open with my thoughts and ideas (except the ones under NDA - those might be trickier to share). Here goes nothing!
